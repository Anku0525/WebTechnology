Subway Surfers is one of the most popular and easiest gameon google play store which is made by Jyoti Dwivedi. This game is tribute to a boy who died while skating in railway tracks. In this game, there are many players who plays a role of young graffiti artist. They begin to run to escape from the polic in the railway tracks. while running, they collects golden coins and other gift item also which help in unlock other players while dodging trains and other object. If they hit with the train more than one time, game will finished and all coins and gift will collect in collection boxes of coins and gift.
